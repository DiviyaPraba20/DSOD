Our classroom has no walls

Enjoy DSODentist original content, on-demand education, and more—right from your smartphone or tablet. By providing 24/7 access to Virtual Events, In-Person Events, and Partner Events, the DSODentist platform supports dental professionals seeking to enhance their knowledge and skills in important topics in dentistry. Our goals:

  • • Provide opportunities for learners to increase their awareness and familiarity with innovative technologies and tools
  • • Unite like-minded professionals who share a passion for lifelong learning
  • • Establish an environment that fosters academic excellence
  • • Promote a continuum of enriching content that will enhance critical thinking, clinical training, and ensure continued professional development

View here on the web or via the DSODentist app on your phone or tablet. All content is reviewed by subject matter experts to ensure its clinical relevance, scientific accuracy, and enduring value.

The continuum will be delivered through a series of engaging webinars as well as didactic “hands-on” training activities at convenient locations across the country. Learning opportunities include live and archived virtual events as well as focused Partner Events conducted by GlaxoSmithKline, Align Technologies, and Nobel Biocare, N.A. in key facets of dentistry.
